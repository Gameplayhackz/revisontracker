      How It Works

      Welcome to the Topic Revision Tracker! Here's how it works:

      1. Start by entering a topic in the input field.
      2. Click the "Add Topic" button to add the topic to your list.
      3. Use the "Revise Topics" button to see which topics need revision today.
      4. Click the "Show All Topics" button to view a list of all your topics.
      5. If you need to change the revision date for a topic, select it from the dropdown and enter a new date.

      The Topic Revision Tracker is based on spaced repetition, a proven method for effective learning. It suggests you revise topics at specific intervals:

      • After 1 day
      • After 3 days
      • After 10 days

      Keep track of your study topics and never miss a revision date with the Topic Revision Tracker!
